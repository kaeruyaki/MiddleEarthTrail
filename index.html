The Middle Earth Trail

You are Frodo, nephew to the eccentric and beloved Bilbo, who vanished years ago on another of his mad adventures. Like all Hobbits, you are a gentle soul, fond of peace, quiet, and good tilled earth. A day filled with six meals, a pint at the local inn, and a bit of pipe-weed is a day well spent. Adventures are nasty, disturbing things that make you late for dinner.

You fondly remember your old friend Gandalf, the wandering wizard whose fireworks were the talk of every party. It was he who sent Bilbo on his great journey, and he who would visit you often in the long years after. He was always fascinated by the simple magic ring Bilbo left behind. But then Gandalf disappeared for many years, and when he returned, the laughter was gone from his eyes. The ring, he explained, was something far more dangerous than a simple trinket.

Before you set out, you must choose your family's background. This choice determines your starting resources.
